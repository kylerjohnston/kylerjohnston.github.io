about

I’m graduating from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign this summer with a Master’s degree in English. My research explores how scholars and students can use code and web technologies to research and teach literary and historical texts. In many ways, the Internet defines the social and spatial qualities of twenty-first century life. How do we best use it to solve real, material problems?

I’m currently seeking employment as a JavaScript developer in the Portland, Maine area.
