about

I recently moved to Maine after graduating from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign with a Master’s degree in English. My research at UIUC explored how scholars and students can use web technologies to research and teach literary and historical texts. In many ways, the Internet defines the social and spatial qualities of twenty-first century life. In my work, I try to find the best ways to use web technologies to solve real, material problems.
