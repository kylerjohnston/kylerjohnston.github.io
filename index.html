about

I’m a freelance, full stack web developer and a graduate student in the English department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign where I research literary-historical trends using quantitative methods such as text-mining and topic modeling.

My passion is solving problems with code. As I reach the end of my Master’s degree program, I’ve decided to leave the world of humanities academia to become a professional web developer. I believe web technologies have the potential to change our society for the better, and I want to be on the front lines spurring that change.
